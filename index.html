Web Developer Ethics

Due to the significant influence of information technology (IT) and its permeation in nearly all aspects of our lives, ethical considerations have become increasingly critical. In the realm of web development, where software can impact millions, it is not just desirable but essential that developers embed ethical principles into their workflows and outputs.

The expansive domain of IT encompasses various fields, but here, we focus on the ethics of software development. As developers, we are responsible for the code we write and the technologies we create. The software we develop should respect user privacy, ensure data security, and strive to be inclusive and accessible to all users.

This website itself is a testament to the pivotal role ethics plays in our professional lives. Conceived as an academic exercise in IT Ethics, the site's architecture has been thoughtfully designed to align with these principles. Credit goes to Professor Myeong Lee for the foundational template and to the robust platforms of Jekyll and GitHub Pages, which have been instrumental technologies in bringing this project to fruition.
